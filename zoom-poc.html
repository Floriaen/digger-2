Zoom POC - Tile Flickering Demonstration

Test 1: Scale BEFORE Drawing (Current Approach)

Tiles rendered with zoom applied to canvas context before drawing

Test 2: Scale AFTER Drawing (Your Suggestion)

Tiles rendered at 1:1, then entire canvas content scaled

Test 3: Snapped Zoom (Our Solution)

Zoom snapped to nearest 0.5 increment (1.0, 1.5, 2.0, 2.5, 3.0)

Observations:

  • At integer zoom (1.0, 2.0, 3.0): All methods look perfect ✓
  • At fractional zoom (1.5, 2.3, 2.7): Test 1 & 2 show gaps/overlaps between tiles
  • At fractional zoom: Test 3 (snapped) always looks perfect ✓
  • Conclusion: Test 1 and 2 produce identical flickering - proving scaling WHEN doesn't matter!
